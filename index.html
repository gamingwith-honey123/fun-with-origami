ORIGAMI AND FACTS

1) CAMEL

INTERSETING FACTS ABOUT A CAMEL:

i) CAMEL COMES FROM A ARABIC WORD MEANING 'BEAUTY'.

ii) Dromedary Camels have only one hump.

iii)Dromedary Camels are also called Arabian camels.

1) CHAMELEON

INTERSETING FACTS ABOUT CHAMELEON

i) ALMOST HALF OF ALL KNOWN SPECIES LIVE IN MADAGASCAR.

ii) SKIN CRYSTALS ENABLE THEM TO CHANGE COLOR AT WILL.

iii) THEY MAINLY CHANGE COLOR IN ORDER TO COMMUNICATE OR REGULATE BODY TEMPERATURE.

1) PIGEON

INTERSETING FACTS ABOUT PIGEON

i) They are one of only a small number of species to pass the ‘mirror test’ – a test of self recognition.

ii) Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

iii) Pigeons and humans have lived in close proximity for thousands of years.

1) TEDDY BEAR

INTERSETING FACTS ABOUT TEDDY BEARS

i) Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.

ii) The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.

iii) September 9th is National Teddy Bear Day!

1) PANDA

INTERSETING FACTS ABOUT PANDA

i) A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear.

ii) ON 17 MARCH PANDA DAY IS CELEBRATED!

iii) Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
